Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect classic Omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional filings: cheese, diced, vegetables, cooked meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non sticky frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omlette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filings(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edge but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of omlette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omlette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge it over the filling. Let it cook for another minute, then side it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with addintional salt and pepper if needed.
